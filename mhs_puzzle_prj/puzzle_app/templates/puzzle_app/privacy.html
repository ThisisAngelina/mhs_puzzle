Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2025

Welcome to My Healthy Skin Diagnostic. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as email addresses or real names. The only information we store is your username and your associated quiz category results. These results help personalize your experience, but they are not linked to any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your username and quiz category results are used solely for:

  • Displaying quiz results
  • Providing a personalized experience based on your quiz category scores

We do not use this data for marketing, analytics, or any third-party tracking.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored and is not shared with third parties. We take reasonable measures to ensure that your information remains safe. Quiz category results are only linked to usernames and not to any personally identifiable information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, third-party tracking, or analytics tools.

5. Your Rights

Since we only store usernames and quiz category results, there is minimal data associated with your account. However, you may request to delete your quiz results by contacting us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
